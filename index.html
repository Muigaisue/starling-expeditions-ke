WHO WE ARE

We Don't Just Organize Trips... We Create Memories.

Starling Expeditions KE is an outdoor adventure company based in Kenya, committed to creating unforgettable experiences through hiking, road trips, waterfall expeditions, camping, team building and tailor-made adventures.

Our mission is simple: Embrace Nature. We believe every adventure should reconnect people with nature, build friendships and leave lasting memories.

Mission

To inspire people to embrace nature through memorable, safe and affordable outdoor adventures.

Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted outdoor adventure company.

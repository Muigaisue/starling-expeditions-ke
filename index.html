ABOUT STARLING

Embrace Nature. Create Memories.

Starling Expeditions KE is a Kenyan adventure company dedicated to creating unforgettable outdoor experiences. We believe nature is the best classroom, the best escape, and the best place to build lasting memories.

From breathtaking mountain hikes and scenic road trips to spectacular waterfalls and tailor-made outdoor experiences, every expedition is designed with safety, professionalism and fun in mind.

Whether you are travelling as an individual, family, institution or corporate group, our mission is to help you explore Kenya like never before.

Our Mission

To inspire people to embrace nature by offering safe, memorable and affordable adventure experiences.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading adventure and nature expedition company through exceptional customer experiences.

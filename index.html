WHO WE ARE

We don't just visit places.
We experience them.

Starling Expeditions KE is an outdoor adventure brand built for people who love the wild. We bring together hikers, explorers and adventure seekers for unforgettable experiences across Kenya.

Whether it is a challenging trail, a scenic road trip or a hidden waterfall, our goal is simple: help you step outside, embrace nature and create stories worth telling.

ABOUT STARLING EXPEDITIONS KE

Explore Kenya.
Embrace Nature.

Starling Expeditions KE was founded with one purpose: bringing people closer to nature through memorable, safe and exciting outdoor experiences.

Whether you are climbing a mountain, discovering hidden waterfalls, enjoying a scenic road trip, or simply spending time outdoors, every expedition is carefully planned to create unforgettable memories.

Our Mission

To inspire people to explore Kenya responsibly while creating memorable outdoor experiences for individuals, institutions and organizations.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted outdoor adventure company by delivering exceptional travel experiences and promoting environmental conservation.
